Fiction Books

It Ends with Us
Author: Colleen Hoover

A deeply emotional contemporary romance that follows Lily Bloom, a young woman navigating love, heartbreak, and difficult choices. When she meets the charming neurosurgeon Ryle Kincaid, their relationship seems perfect—until painful truths from the past resurface. The novel explores love, resilience, and the courage to break cycles of abuse.

The Maze Runner
Author: James Dashner

In a post-apocalyptic world, Thomas wakes up in a mysterious maze with no memory of his past. Along with other boys, he must solve the puzzles and survive the dangers of the maze.

Romance Books

The Notebook

A heartwarming tale of love and loss, following the story of Noah and Allie as they navigate the challenges of their relationship across different stages of life.
